## BCS PRE-LEAVE CHECKLIST (NO-DISPENSE VERSION)

### 1. Snapshot
- `snapshot`  
- Confirm:
  - Current image received
  - Correct framing

### 2. Honey System (no movement)
- Visual check:
  - Tubing fully connected end-to-end
  - Continuous honey column (no air gaps)
  - Syringe seated correctly in mount
  - Plunger aligned (not slipping or skewed)

### 3. Kibble
- `drop 1`  
- Confirm:
  - Exactly 1 bin dispensed
  - Full clean rotation (no hesitation)

### 4. Supplies
- Kibble:
  - Enough (>2× expected need)
  - No tunneling / bridging
- Honey:
  - Enough volume
  - Fully primed (no air gaps)

### 5. Physical
- Actuator mount rigid
- Tubing secure
- Wires not under tension
- Nothing can be bumped out of place

---

### GO / NO-GO
- **GO only if all checks are clean and unambiguous**
- Any ambiguity = **NO-GO**
